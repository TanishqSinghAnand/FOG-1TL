The symptons of novel coronavirus are :
  1. Cough
  2. fever
  3. tiredness
  4. (In severe cases) difficulty in breathing
Precautionary Measures :
1. Wash hand more often
Wash your hands for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub
2. While coughing cover your mouth
Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze
3. Dont touch your face
Touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean
4. Keep distance between each preson
Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell
5. STAY HOME
Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
